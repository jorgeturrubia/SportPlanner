# SportPlanner Product Overview

## Core Value Proposition
SportPlanner is a comprehensive sports training planning application that streamlines the coaching process by enabling coaches to create training objectives, design structured sessions, and monitor training execution with minimal effort. The platform combines individual coaching tools with a collaborative marketplace where coaches can share plannings, exercises, and receive community ratings up to 5 stars, creating a quick setup solution for complete training management.

## Target Users & Use Cases

### Primary Users
- **Individual Coaches**: Create and manage training sessions for their teams, access exercise libraries, and utilize concept-based training methodologies
- **Sports Directors**: Oversee multiple teams within clubs, manage coaching staff, and coordinate organization-wide training strategies  
- **Club/Organization Administrators**: Handle user management, subscription oversight, and organizational structure across multiple teams and coaches
- **Assistant Coaches & Staff Members**: Support lead coaches with session planning, execution monitoring, and administrative tasks

### User Roles & Permissions
- **Administrator**: Full system access and user management
- **Director**: Multi-team oversight with extended functionality
- **Coach**: Team-specific access with full training capabilities
- Permission-based team assignments with privacy controls

## Subscription Tiers

### Free Tier (€0)
- Access to 1 team
- Maximum 15 training sessions
- Basic functionality for trial users

### Entrenador (Coach) Tier
- Full access to all coaching features
- Unlimited training sessions
- Custom concept creation and management
- Individual coach focused functionality

### Club Tier
- Multiple team management
- Director mode with organizational oversight
- Extended functionality for club operations
- Multi-user coordination tools

## Key Features

### Authentication & User Management
- Supabase-based authentication system
- Multi-tier subscription management
- Role-based access control with granular permissions
- Complex user-subscription-team-planning relationship structure

### Team & Training Management
- Comprehensive team setup (gender, age category, skill levels A/B/C)
- Concept-based training system with hierarchical categories/subcategories
- Exercise library with direct concept linking
- Itineraries for rapid planning deployment
- Session management with dynamic view and integrated timer functionality

### Planning & Execution Tools
- Structured training session creation
- Real-time session monitoring and execution control
- Reports and calendar functionality for tracking progress
- Quick setup workflows requiring minimal user interaction

### Marketplace & Collaboration
- Community-driven marketplace for sharing training plannings
- Exercise and planning import/export functionality
- 5-star rating system for community content
- Coach-to-coach knowledge sharing platform

### Privacy & Security
- Multi-user privacy controls
- Permission-based content sharing
- Secure user data management

## Business Objectives

### Growth Metrics
- **User Acquisition**: Increase monthly sign-up rate by targeting coaching communities and sports organizations
- **Market Penetration**: Establish presence in amateur and semi-professional sports coaching segments

### Revenue Optimization
- **Subscription Conversion**: Convert free tier users to paid subscriptions through value demonstration
- **Customer Retention**: Maintain high weekly and monthly active user rates through engaging features

### Platform Engagement
- **Content Creation**: Drive marketplace activity through user-generated training content and community interactions
- **Coach-User Relationships**: Facilitate meaningful connections between coaches and their teams

## User Experience Flow

### Coach Onboarding
1. Authentication via Supabase
2. Subscription tier selection
3. Team creation and configuration
4. Initial planning setup using itineraries or custom concepts

### Daily Training Management
1. Session creation using concept-based approach
2. Exercise selection from integrated library
3. Real-time session execution with timer functionality
4. Post-training reporting and calendar updates

### Marketplace Interaction
1. Browse community-shared plannings and exercises
2. Import relevant content for adaptation
3. Share successful training approaches
4. Rate and review community contributions

## Success Metrics

### User Growth & Engagement
- **User Acquisition**: Number of new sign-ups per month
- **Active User Retention**: Weekly and monthly active users tracking
- **Platform Engagement**: Training sessions created and completed counts

### Revenue Performance
- **Subscription Conversion Rate**: Free to paid tier conversion percentage
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Long-term subscription retention and upgrades

### Community Health
- **Marketplace Activity**: Transaction volume and coach-user interaction frequency
- **Content Quality**: User satisfaction through feedback ratings and Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Volume and quality of shared plannings and exercises

## Strategic Positioning
SportPlanner positions itself as the comprehensive solution for modern sports coaching, bridging individual coaching needs with community collaboration while maintaining simplicity and efficiency in daily training management workflows.
