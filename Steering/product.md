# SportPlanner Product Overview

## Core Value Proposition
SportPlanner is a comprehensive sports training planning application that empowers coaches and sports directors to create structured training objectives, design effective sessions, control training execution, and share proven methodologies through a collaborative marketplace.

## Target Users & Use Cases
- **Primary Users - Coaches & Sports Directors**: Create training plans, manage teams, track progress, and execute training sessions with real-time control and analytics
- **Secondary Users - Assistant Coaches**: Added users with specific permissions (view-only, edit-only, or team-specific access) to support training execution and planning
- **Marketplace Users**: Coaches sharing and discovering training plans through a 5-star rating system for community-driven improvement

## Key Features
1. **Supabase Authentication**: Secure user management with role-based access control
2. **Tiered Subscription Model**: Free (1 team, 15 sessions), Coach (unlimited), Club (multi-team management)
3. **Role-Based Team Access**: Granular permissions for team-specific access with all-access, view-only, or edit-only levels
4. **Concept & Exercise Management**: Organized library with categories for training components
5. **Itineraries for Quick Setup**: Pre-built training program templates for rapid deployment
6. **Auto-Session Generation**: AI-powered session creation based on defined training objectives
7. **Dynamic Training View**: Real-time training execution interface with integrated timer functionality
8. **Reports & Analytics**: Comprehensive tracking and analysis of training progress and outcomes
9. **Training Marketplace**: Community platform for sharing, rating, and discovering training plans

## Business Objectives
- **User Acquisition**: Growing the overall user base through organic growth and referrals
- **Subscription Conversion**: Converting free-tier users to paid Coach and Club subscriptions
- **Revenue Growth**: Increasing paid subscriber count while maintaining user satisfaction
- **Market Leadership**: Establishing SportPlanner as the go-to tool for sports directors and coaches

## User Experience Flow
1. **Quick Onboarding**: Users sign up and can create a complete training program with just a few clicks using itineraries
2. **Planning Phase**: Coaches define objectives, select exercises from the concept library, and build structured training sessions
3. **Execution Phase**: Real-time training view with timer controls for seamless session management
4. **Analysis Phase**: Post-training reports and analytics for continuous improvement
5. **Community Engagement**: Share successful training plans and discover new methodologies through the marketplace

## Success Metrics
- **Total Active Users**: Overall platform adoption and engagement
- **Paid Subscribers**: Number of users on Coach and Club subscription tiers
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of free users upgrading to paid subscriptions
- **Session Completion Rate**: Training execution success and user engagement
- **Marketplace Activity**: Training plan shares, downloads, and ratings
- **User Retention**: Long-term user engagement and subscription renewals
